Gourmet
Goodies

Brand Design & Recipe App


Gourmet goodies is a test of effective UI and UX design with an attention to information hierarchy and a delicate balance of function and form. The app is designed not only to make fine dining dishes easily prep-arable at home but to also compile shopping lists and custom curated anthologies of your favourite recipes.The branding of gourmet goodies leans more to a simple, editorial like digital translation of your modern cookbook with our signature bamboo studio visual style of bright and vibrant colours paired with bold typefaces. The structure of information, navigation menus and iconography make this app easy to use and a joy to cook with.
